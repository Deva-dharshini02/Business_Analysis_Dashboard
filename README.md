**📊 Business Analytics Dashboard**
**📌 Project Overview**
The Business Analytics Dashboard is an interactive Power BI dashboard designed to provide comprehensive insights into business performance. It helps stakeholders monitor key metrics such as sales, profit, quantity sold, discounts, regional performance, and category-wise contributions.

The dashboard transforms raw business data into meaningful visualizations, enabling informed decision-making and performance tracking.

**🎯 Objectives**
Analyze overall business performance.
Monitor sales and profit trends over time.
Identify high-performing regions and categories.
Evaluate the impact of discounts on profitability.
Support strategic business decisions through data visualization.

**📊 Dashboard Features**
**1. Sales Overview**
Displays total sales generated by the business.
Provides a quick summary of revenue performance.
**2. Profit Analysis**
Tracks overall profit earned.
Helps evaluate business profitability.
**3. Quantity Tracking**
Monitors the total quantity of products sold.
**4. Discount Monitoring**
Analyzes total discounts offered.
Assesses the effect of discounts on revenue and profit.
**5. Regional Profit Performance**
Compares profit contribution across different regions:
South
East
West
Central
**6. Monthly Sales Trend**
Visualizes month-wise sales performance.
Identifies seasonal trends and business fluctuations.
**7. Category-wise Sales & Profit Analysis**
Examines contribution from:
Furniture
Office Supplies
Technology
**8. Sales Share by Category**
Shows percentage contribution of each category to overall sales.
**9. Sales vs Profit Analysis**
Analyzes relationship between sales and profit across sub-categories.
Helps identify profitable and underperforming products.
**10. Interactive Filters**
Users can filter data by:
Month
Category
Region
**🔍 Key Insights**
South region contributes the highest profit.
Technology category generates strong sales performance.
Sales peak during July and November.
Certain sub-categories generate high sales but comparatively lower profit.
Discounts have a direct impact on profitability and should be optimized.
